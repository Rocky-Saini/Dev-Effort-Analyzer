About DevEffort

What is DevEffort?

DevEffort is an AI-powered tool that helps developers generate professional Work Breakdown Structure (WBS) and Impact Analysis documents in minutes instead of hours.

It scans your codebase, understands your architecture, reads your Jira story, and uses Kiro AI to produce meaningful, email-ready documents that your team and management expect.

The Problem We Solve

Every sprint, developers spend 2-3 hours per ticket writing WBS and Impact Analysis documents. This is repetitive, time-consuming, and takes away from actual development work.

DevEffort automates this entire process — giving you back those hours to focus on what matters: writing code.

How It Works

1
Point to your repo

Select your local project path. DevEffort scans controllers, services, entities, events, and configs.

2
Paste your Jira story

Include the story ID, description, acceptance criteria, and any technical notes.

3
Add your context

Any additional notes — migrations, known impacts, dependencies you're aware of.

4
Kiro generates everything

AI builds a refined prompt from your inputs + codebase analysis, then generates WBS and Impact Analysis.
